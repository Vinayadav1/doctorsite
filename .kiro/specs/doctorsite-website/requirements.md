# Requirements Document

## Introduction

DoctorSite is a premium website development service that specializes in building personal websites and online presence for doctors and surgeons. The platform aims to help medical professionals attract more patients, build trust, and establish a professional digital presence through modern, conversion-focused websites.

## Glossary

- **DoctorSite_Platform**: The complete website system including all pages, components, and features
- **Hero_Section**: The primary above-the-fold section on the homepage with headline and CTA
- **CTA_Button**: Call-to-action button designed to drive user conversions (booking calls, contact)
- **Navigation_Bar**: The sticky header navigation component
- **Service_Card**: A UI component displaying individual service offerings
- **Pricing_Plan**: A structured package offering with features and pricing
- **Portfolio_Item**: A showcase entry displaying doctor website examples or case studies
- **Contact_Form**: The form component for collecting user inquiries
- **Testimonial_Component**: A UI element displaying client feedback
- **Trust_Section**: A section highlighting credibility and experience
- **Responsive_Layout**: Design that adapts to different screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **Glass_Effect**: A glassmorphism UI style with transparency and blur
- **Smooth_Animation**: Transition effects including scroll, fade-in, and hover states

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Homepage Hero Section

**User Story:** As a visiting doctor, I want to immediately understand what DoctorSite offers, so that I can decide if this service is relevant to my practice.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Hero_Section SHALL display the headline "Build Your Digital Presence as a Doctor"
2. THE Hero_Section SHALL display a subheading explaining patient growth and trust benefits
3. THE Hero_Section SHALL include a CTA_Button labeled "Book Free Consultation"
4. WHEN a user clicks the CTA_Button, THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL navigate to the contact form or booking interface
5. THE Hero_Section SHALL use premium visual styling with soft gradients and clean medical aesthetics

### Requirement 2: Trust and Credibility Display

**User Story:** As a doctor evaluating the service, I want to see proof of experience and credibility, so that I can trust DoctorSite with my professional presence.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Trust_Section SHALL display experience with Pristyn Care in a subtle, professional manner
2. THE Trust_Section SHALL be positioned prominently on the homepage after the Hero_Section
3. THE Trust_Section SHALL use trust-building visual elements such as logos, statistics, or credentials

### Requirement 3: Benefits Presentation

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to understand the specific benefits of having a professional website, so that I can see the value proposition.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL display a benefits section on the homepage
2. THE Benefits_Section SHALL highlight "Get more patients from Google" as a benefit
3. THE Benefits_Section SHALL highlight "Build trust with professional presence" as a benefit
4. THE Benefits_Section SHALL highlight "Online appointment booking" as a benefit
5. THE Benefits_Section SHALL use visual icons or graphics to enhance each benefit

### Requirement 4: Services Overview

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to see what services are offered, so that I can understand the complete offering.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a Services page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Website Development for Doctors" as a Service_Card
3. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Personal Branding" as a Service_Card
4. THE Services_Page SHALL display "SEO Optimization (Google ranking)" as a Service_Card
5. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Online Appointment System" as a Service_Card
6. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Video Editing (for reels, YouTube)" as a Service_Card
7. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Social Media Management" as a Service_Card
8. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Digital Marketing" as a Service_Card
9. THE Services_Page SHALL display "Content Writing (medical blogs)" as a Service_Card
10. THE Homepage SHALL display a preview of services with a link to the full Services page

### Requirement 5: Process Visualization

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to understand how the service works step-by-step, so that I know what to expect if I sign up.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a "How We Work" page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 1: "Consultation Call"
3. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 2: "Requirement Understanding"
4. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 3: "Design & Development"
5. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 4: "Revisions"
6. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 5: "Launch"
7. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL display Step 6: "Ongoing Support"
8. THE How_We_Work_Page SHALL present steps in a visual timeline or step-by-step UI format
9. THE Homepage SHALL display a short version of the process flow

### Requirement 6: Pricing Plans

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to see clear pricing options, so that I can choose a plan that fits my budget and needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a Pricing page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE Pricing_Page SHALL display three Pricing_Plans: "Basic", "Professional", and "Premium"
3. THE Basic_Plan SHALL be positioned as suitable for starter doctors
4. THE Professional_Plan SHALL be marked as "Most Popular" or "Most Recommended"
5. THE Premium_Plan SHALL be positioned as full branding and marketing solution
6. EACH Pricing_Plan SHALL display a features list
7. EACH Pricing_Plan SHALL display pricing information (monthly or one-time)
8. THE Professional_Plan SHALL have visual highlighting to indicate recommendation

### Requirement 7: Portfolio and Case Studies

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to see examples of previous work, so that I can evaluate the quality and results.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a Portfolio page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE Portfolio_Page SHALL display multiple Portfolio_Items showcasing doctor websites
3. WHERE available, THE Portfolio_Item SHALL display before/after transformation visuals
4. THE Portfolio_Page SHALL include case studies focusing on patient growth metrics
5. EACH Portfolio_Item SHALL present results in a trust-building manner

### Requirement 8: Contact and Lead Capture

**User Story:** As a doctor interested in the service, I want multiple ways to contact DoctorSite, so that I can easily get in touch.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a Contact page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE Contact_Page SHALL display a Contact_Form with fields for name, email, phone, and message
3. THE Contact_Page SHALL display a WhatsApp CTA_Button
4. THE Contact_Page SHALL display a "Book Free Call" CTA_Button
5. WHEN a user submits the Contact_Form, THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL validate all required fields
6. WHEN form validation passes, THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL submit the inquiry data

### Requirement 9: Navigation and Site Structure

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want easy navigation across all pages, so that I can find information quickly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Navigation_Bar SHALL remain sticky at the top during page scrolling
2. THE Navigation_Bar SHALL include links to: Home, About Us, Services, How We Work, Pricing, Portfolio, Contact, and Blog
3. THE Navigation_Bar SHALL be accessible on all pages
4. THE Navigation_Bar SHALL include prominent CTA_Button placement
5. WHILE scrolling down, THE Navigation_Bar SHALL remain visible and functional

### Requirement 10: About Us Page

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to learn about the team and company behind DoctorSite, so that I can build confidence in the service.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include an About Us page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE About_Us_Page SHALL explain the company mission and vision
3. THE About_Us_Page SHALL highlight relevant experience in the medical industry
4. THE About_Us_Page SHALL use trust-building language and visuals

### Requirement 11: Testimonials Display

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to read testimonials from other doctors, so that I can see social proof of the service quality.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Homepage SHALL include a testimonials section
2. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL display multiple Testimonial_Components
3. EACH Testimonial_Component SHALL be focused on doctor experiences
4. THE Testimonials_Section SHALL use credible presentation with names and specialties where available

### Requirement 12: Blog Structure

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want access to helpful content and resources, so that I can learn more about digital presence for doctors.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include a Blog page accessible from the Navigation_Bar
2. THE Blog_Page SHALL display blog post listings
3. THE Blog_Page SHALL support navigation to individual blog posts
4. THE Blog content SHALL focus on topics relevant to doctors building online presence

### Requirement 13: Responsive Design

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want the website to work perfectly on my device, so that I can browse comfortably.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL implement Responsive_Layout for all pages
2. THE Responsive_Layout SHALL adapt to mobile screen sizes (320px to 768px)
3. THE Responsive_Layout SHALL adapt to tablet screen sizes (768px to 1024px)
4. THE Responsive_Layout SHALL adapt to desktop screen sizes (1024px and above)
5. WHEN viewed on mobile, THE Navigation_Bar SHALL display a hamburger menu
6. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL prioritize mobile-first design approach

### Requirement 14: Visual Design System

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want a premium, cohesive visual experience, so that I perceive DoctorSite as professional and trustworthy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL use a color palette of light blue, teal, white, and soft gradients
2. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL use modern fonts such as Inter or Poppins
3. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL apply rounded corners to UI components
4. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL apply soft shadows to elevated elements
5. WHERE appropriate, THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL use Glass_Effect styling
6. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL maintain consistent spacing and visual hierarchy across all pages

### Requirement 15: Animations and Interactions

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want smooth, modern interactions, so that the website feels premium and engaging.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL implement Smooth_Animation for scroll events
2. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL implement fade-in animations for content appearing on scroll
3. WHEN a user hovers over interactive elements, THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL display hover effects
4. THE Smooth_Animation SHALL enhance user experience without causing performance issues
5. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL ensure animations are smooth at 60fps

### Requirement 16: Conversion Optimization

**User Story:** As the business owner, I want the website to maximize lead generation, so that we convert visitors into clients.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include CTA_Buttons on every page
2. THE Homepage SHALL include a final CTA section before the footer
3. THE CTA_Buttons SHALL use action-oriented language focused on booking or contacting
4. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL use conversion-focused copywriting throughout
5. THE Copy SHALL be written in simple English avoiding technical jargon
6. THE Copy SHALL focus on results such as "more patients" and "better reputation"

### Requirement 17: Performance and SEO

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want the website to load quickly, so that I don't abandon it due to slow performance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL load initial page content within 3 seconds on standard broadband
2. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL implement SEO-friendly HTML structure with semantic tags
3. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL include proper meta tags for each page
4. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL optimize images for web delivery
5. THE DoctorSite_Platform SHALL implement lazy loading for below-the-fold content
